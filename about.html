About me

GH Front-end Developer, based in Accra, Ghana


Greetings! I'm Sarah Duker, a dedicated professional with a passion for customer service and a budding interest in technology. I bring a wealth of experience from my roles in call centers and tech support, coupled with ongoing learning in coding through the SheCodes program.

My journey started in the world of commerce, where I earned a Bachelor's degree in Accounting and Finance from the University of Cape Coast. Over the years, I've honed my skills in customer service, communication, and problem-solving, earning accolades for delivering exceptional experiences.

Currently enrolled in SheCodes, I am venturing into the dynamic realm of technology. My projects showcase a fusion of creativity and coding, notably the Weather App hosted on GitHub and Vercel.

I thrive on challenges, value collaboration, and possess a keen eye for detail. Whether it's resolving technical issues or planning committee activities, my approach is characterized by professionalism and a commitment to excellence.

Join me on this exciting journey of exploration and growth. Let's connect, collaborate, and make meaningful contributions together!
